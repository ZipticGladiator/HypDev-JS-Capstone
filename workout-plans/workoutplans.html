**Full Body Routine 1**

Exercise Sets Reps Comments
Squats 3 8-10 Use proper form and go as low as you can without pain.
Bench press 3 8-10 Make sure to use a spotter and keep your elbows tucked in.
Deadlifts 3 8-10 Use proper form and engage your core throughout the lift.
Shoulder press 3 10-12 Keep your back straight and engage your core throughout the lift.
Bicep curls 3 10-12 Use a weight that challenges you but allows you to complete the full set.
Tricep extensions 3 10-12 Keep your elbows tucked in and engage your triceps throughout the lift.
Plank 3 30-60 seconds Maintain a straight line from your head to your toes and engage your core throughout.
